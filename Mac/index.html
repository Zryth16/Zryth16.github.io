About Us

At TruGreen Grounds Maintenance, we’re more than just a grounds maintenance company—we’re a team of dedicated professionals who care about the appearance, health, and longevity of every outdoor space we touch. Since our founding, we’ve built a reputation for reliable service, expert care, and a hands-on approach that puts our clients first.

With years of experience in the industry, we’ve worked with a wide range of properties—from private homes and apartment complexes to office parks, schools, and municipal grounds. No matter the size or scope, our goal remains the same: to deliver top-quality maintenance that keeps your property looking its best, season after season.

We combine traditional values like hard work and attention to detail with modern tools and environmentally conscious practices. Our team is trained, courteous, and committed to doing the job right the first time. Whether you need weekly lawn care, hedge trimming, weed control, or full-scale landscape management, we’re here to make your life easier and your property stand out.

At the heart of what we do is a simple belief: well-maintained grounds create better spaces—for living, for working, and for the community.
